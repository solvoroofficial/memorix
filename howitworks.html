How Memorix Works

Step 1 — Enter Your Information

Provide your name, favorite hobby, lucky number, and an optional special character to personalize your password generation process.

Step 2 — Generate Passwords

Click on the “Generate Passwords” button to instantly receive multiple password suggestions based on your inputs.

Step 3 — Copy & Use

Select your preferred password and copy it with one click — ready to use across your accounts.

Step 4 — Keep It Secure

Regularly update your passwords, avoid reusing the same ones, and keep them stored securely or memorized for better safety.
