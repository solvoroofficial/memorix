How Memorix Works

Step 1: Enter Your Info

Provide simple personal details like your name, hobbies, favorite numbers, and an optional symbol. This allows Memorix to create passwords that are unique and easy to remember for you.

Step 2: Generate Passwords

Click the "Generate Passwords" button. Memorix will produce multiple strong yet memorable passwords based on your inputs, giving you several options to choose from.

Step 3: Copy & Use

Select the password you like the most and copy it using the convenient copy button. You can now safely use it across your accounts, knowing it’s both secure and memorable.

Step 4: Tips for Security

To ensure maximum security, we recommend periodically updating your passwords and not reusing them across multiple accounts. Memorix makes it easy to generate new memorable passwords whenever you need them.
